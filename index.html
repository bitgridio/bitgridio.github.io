Welcome to the BitGrid.io

This space is dedicated to my experiments in Virtual Reality, mostly with Janus VR which is a new browser (like Chrome or Firefox) specifically for VR content.

If you view this site with Janus, you will see something like the picture in the background, but in three dimensional space, with the links appearing as portals to other sites (or "rooms") that you can walk and fly through. Download Janus today and come join us!
